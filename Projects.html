  • BWB prosthetic socket team (Winter - Spring 2017)
    @BWB Club(Bioengineers Without Border)

  • Bechdel Test (Spring 2017)
    @INFO 201 Class Final Project

    The Bechdel Test assesses whether a movie has at least two female characters with names, whether they talked to each other, and whether their topic is about men. Many major films failed this test. My group wanted to raise social awareness of female role and we presented the analysis using R and Shiny. We made interactive data visualizations and gave conclusion of what we found. My work was the home page, the year made page, the search page, the about us page and I added CSS stylesheet.   • Kill the Cup Challenge (Autumn 2015)
    @SEED (Student Expressing Environmental Dedication)

    Kill the Cup Challenge is a nationwise competition that counts the percent of disposable cups usage in school cafe. After a month of advertising and tabling, University of Washington ranked top 2 among 16 universities around the nation. I also helped organize many other similar events in SEED, such as "the one thing challenge" which encourages students to do at least one thing that's environmental-friendly and visiting World's greenest building "the Bullitt Center".
